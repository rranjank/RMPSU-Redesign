Admissions 2026–27

Applications are now open. Join RMPSU and become part of a legacy of excellence.

Apply Now View Programs

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to complete your admission application for 2026–27.

1

Register Online

Create your account on the RMPSU Admission Portal using your valid email ID and mobile number.

Go to Portal →
2

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with your personal, academic and course preference details.

3

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of mark sheets, photo, signature, ID proof and caste certificate.

4

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee online via Net Banking, UPI or Debit/Credit Card.

General: ₹500 | SC/ST: ₹250

Applications Closing Soon!

Last date to apply: 31 July 2026. Don't miss your chance to join RMPSU.

Start Application

WHO CAN APPLY

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate (UG)

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST)
  • Age: 17 years as on 1st July 2026
  • Subject-specific requirements as per programme

Postgraduate (PG)

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate (45% for SC/ST)
  • Must appear in RMPSU PG Entrance Test
  • For LL.M: LL.B. with 55% marks required

Ph.D. Programme

  • Master's degree with minimum 55% marks
  • Qualify UGC-NET / GATE / RMPSU Entrance
  • Research proposal submission required
  • Interview shortlisting based on merit

Documents Required

Keep the following documents ready in PDF/JPG format before starting your application.

Required Documents
  • 10th Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • 12th / Qualifying Exam Mark Sheet
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Character Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for fee waiver)
  • Aadhaar Card / Valid ID Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Scanned Signature
Note: All documents must be self-attested. Originals will be verified at the time of admission.
