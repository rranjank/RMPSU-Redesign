WELCOME TO RMPSU

A Legacy of Excellence in Higher Education

Raja Mahendra Pratap Singh University, Aligarh was established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in 2021.Named in honour of the great freedom fighter and social reformer Raja Mahendra Pratap Singh, the university strives to provide quality higher education to students across the region, fostering research, innovation, and inclusive growth.

It is a privilege and responsibility to serve as the Vice-Chancellor of Raja Mahendra Pratap Singh University, Aligarh. Our university remains committed to academic excellence, quality research and transparent governance.
